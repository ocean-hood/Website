About the Project

The Ocean Garden project is a cause that we, a group of aspiring young students from various fields of study, have been working on to raise awareness about the urgent need for radical changes in society regarding ocean microbes and the vital role of national and international governance. Our aim is to find a noble solution to this ongoing issue, and we hope that our efforts will make a difference.
